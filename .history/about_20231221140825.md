# Projects
## Semantic Segmentation on Radar Point Cloud
During my master's research, I focus on semantic segmentation in sparse and irregular radar point clouds, a challenging task. I proposed the Spatial and Temporal Awareness Network (STA-Net), a novel solution processing multi-scan data into a single frame, preserving temporal features. The introduction of a prompt layer substantially enhanced the network's performance, particularly in segmenting small objects like pedestrians. This master's work represents a notable stride in advancing safety and efficiency in autonomous vehicle technology.
